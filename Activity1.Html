Dubai Scholars Private School

Dubai Scholars Private School, located in Al Qusais, Dubai, is one of the oldest and most prestigious educational institutions in the UAE. Founded in 1976, the school is managed by the Scholars International Group and offers the National Curriculum of England to students from FS1 to Year 1312.

Dubai Scholars emphasizes core values such as integrity, empathy, respect, resilience, and humility. The school includes state-of-the-art facilities designed to support a well-rounded education, including a swimming pool, multi-sports field, and zoned playgrounds.
Dubai Scholars has a strong multicultural community and is recognized by leading accreditation bodies such as the KHDA, BSO, and British Schools of the Middle East..
As of the 2024-2025 academic year, Dubai Scholars Private School has an enrollment of over 2,350 students.
The school is registered with the British Schools Overseas, which is the overseas accrediting body of the Department for Education of England and Wales.
